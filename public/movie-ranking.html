Top Movies of All Time According to Opeyemi Salami

My top three movies of all time

Jumong

This was my first koren movie. I can still remember me an my siblings running out in the middle of the night to go get the next episode of the movie; we were so in love with it

Kingsmen

I can watch this movie over and again without getting any tired. From the story, to the action, kingsmen was just a top tier movie

Abejoye (The King maker)

This is one of my best african movie, it shows african power at its best and also emphasises how the power of Jesus is greater than any other power
